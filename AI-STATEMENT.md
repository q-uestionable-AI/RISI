## Personal statement on AI-assisted engineering

This project uses a human-led, AI-accelerated workflow. AI assistants may propose plans, operate the
harness, implement and review code, verify evidence, and assist with interpretation. This is an
intentional demonstration of what a human researcher and frontier AI systems can produce together.

Human approval governs scope, authorization, releases, and research claims. Models are treated as
untrusted callers at the harness boundary, and their interpretations do not replace deterministic
evidence, reproducibility, technical judgment, or human accountability.
